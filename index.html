The Benefits

There are many benefits to eating metal. Pewter will make you tougher, tin can sharpen your eyesight, steel will let you soar through the air, and those are just the basics! Just wait until you’re Rioting crowds to do your bidding, felling buildings with duralumin-enhanced Steelpushing, or controlling time itself!

How to Apply

I hear you asking - “How do I get this power?” Well, the easiest way is to be born a Mistborn and Snap at a young age, but barring that, the only way is through the Steel Ministry! The long way is to join up, work your way up to High Obligator of a Canton, and be selected by the Lord Ruler Himself to be given Lerasium (act fast though - supplies are very limited). The quick way is much easier. Simply try and impress the Steel Inquisitors, and they’ll perform the mysterious process to make you one of them (not for those who are squeamish around large nails). If you’d like to take this route, don’t worry - they’ll find you.
